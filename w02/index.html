Steve Lee

INTERVIEW by Emma Burke

What music do you like to listen to while working?

Movie soundtracks. They don't have lyrics so they're less distracting.

Name three designers whose work you find inspiring.

  • David Carson
  • Vaughn Oliver
  • Park Si Young

Name something (a format, an item) you want to learn how to design but haven't had the chance to try to yet.

Figma, which is an AI based tool for creating websites. A lot of companies want employees who know how to use this tool so it's worth learning.

If you were a font, what would you be (you can't say helvetica or comic sans-- both jokes are played). Explain why?

ITC Benguiat (strangers things font, but not because of stranger things. It's retro)

Name one website that you rely on (try to avoid a platform like google/amazon/pinterest/etc).

Fonts in use (

typography website, shows typographic elements in posters, movie titles, magazines, with info a out typefaces used, has bonus material like interviews and comments)

We've talked a bit about film. Are you interested in filmmaking and/ordesigning for film?

Yes, wants to do filmmaking in the future. Likes making designs related to film (title sequences, posters, trailers) Would design a title sequence for Marty Supreme, different direction for weird title sequence, ping pong focused
