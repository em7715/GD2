Same movie, different poster.

Just like book covers, film posters have alternate designs-- sometimes drastic changes, sometimes subtle--when they arrive in a new country and their film market to reflect that region’s sociocultural context and appeal more specifically to those people. Changes in film poster design can reflect the politcal state of the country, the country’s biases or beliefs or values, or can tell us something about what content those people gravitate towards.
